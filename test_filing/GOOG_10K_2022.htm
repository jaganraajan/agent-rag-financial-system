PART I

Item 1. Business

Overview

Alphabet Inc. ("Alphabet", "the Company", "we", "us" or "our") is a collection of companies, with Google being the largest. We report our segment results as Google Services, Google Cloud, and Other Bets:

  • Google Services includes products and services such as ads, Android, Chrome, hardware, Google Maps, Google Play, Search, and YouTube. Google Services generates revenues primarily from advertising; sales of apps and in-app purchases, and hardware; and fees received for subscription-based products such as YouTube Premium and YouTube TV.
  • Google Cloud includes Google's infrastructure and platform services, collaboration tools, and other services for enterprise customers. Google Cloud generates revenues from fees received for Google Cloud Platform services, Google Workspace communication and collaboration tools, and other enterprise services.
  • Other Bets includes businesses such as Access, Calico, CapitalG, GV, Verily, Waymo, and X. Other Bets generates revenues primarily from the sale of internet and TV services through Access, autonomous vehicle technology development through Waymo, and health technology and services through Verily.

Our Mission

Our mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful. We are committed to significantly improving the lives of as many people as possible. We aspire to create services so useful people come to rely on them over time.

Products and Services

Google Services

Google Services includes our main Google products and services that are primarily consumer-facing. These include:

Search and other: We provide Search globally in more than 150 languages. Billions of people globally rely on Search to find information. Our other products include Google Images, Google News, Google Shopping, Google Flights, and Google Finance.

YouTube: YouTube is a global platform for video sharing and consumption that allows people to discover, watch and share video content. YouTube has over 2 billion logged-in monthly users.

Google Ads: Google Ads enables businesses to reach potential customers at moments that matter. We offer a range of advertising solutions across Google properties and partner sites.

Android: Android is an open-source mobile operating system that we make available to original equipment manufacturers and developers globally.

Google Play: Google Play is our app and digital content store where users can discover and download applications, games, music, movies, TV shows, books and other digital content.

Chrome: Chrome is a fast, secure, and free web browser built for the modern web.

Item 1A. Risk Factors

Risks Related to Our Business and Industry

We face intense competition: We operate in highly competitive markets. We face competition from general purpose search engines and information services, as well as companies that provide answers to users' questions in specific subject areas or verticals.

Privacy and data protection laws: We are subject to complex and evolving privacy and data protection laws and regulations in the U.S. and internationally. These laws may limit our ability to collect, use, process, and share personal information.

Cybersecurity threats: We regularly face cybersecurity threats including attempts to gain unauthorized access to our systems and data. A successful cyber attack could result in theft of our intellectual property or user data.

Algorithm changes: Changes to our search and other algorithms could impact our traffic and revenue. Publishers and advertisers continuously monitor these changes and may modify their behavior in response.
